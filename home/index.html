Three Core Principles of DMCA Content Protection:

Copyright — Original Content Protection

Copyright safeguards creative works such as images, videos, text, and music. Unauthorized use or reproduction without the creator’s consent may result in limited access or removal. Always ensure that your content is original or properly licensed.

Intellectual Property — Creative Ownership

Intellectual property covers ideas, inventions, and designs that belong to their creators. Using such materials without permission can lead to enforcement actions. Respect originality and credit rightful owners when using external assets.

Trademark — Brand and Identity Protection

Trademarks protect names, logos, and symbols representing a brand or organization. Using a registered mark without authorization may mislead users or infringe upon legal rights. Always verify authorization before including brand elements in your ads or materials.
